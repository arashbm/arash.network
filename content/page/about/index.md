Hi, I'm *Arash Badie-Modiri*. I'm a Candidate for the degree of *Doctor of
Science in Technology* (DSc. Tech.) at [Aalto University][aalto]. I study
[Complex Systems][complex]: systems composed of a large number of components
that interact with each other, such as neurons in a human brains, people in a
social network or buses in a public transport. These systems show behaviour
that emerge only when large number of these components interact.

Recently, I have been working on understanding systems that evolve through time
([temporal networks][temporal]) and how an effect (such as a communicable
disease or a [meme][meme]) spreads in a system.
